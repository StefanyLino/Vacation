ABOUT CARAGUATATUBA:

Caraguatatuba, located on São Paulo's north coast, is known for its beautiful beaches, lush mountains, and vibrant atmosphere. Popular for water sports, hiking, and local cuisine, it’s a favorite spot for nature lovers and beachgoers alike.

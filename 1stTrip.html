On my first trip, I went to Porto de Galinhas, in Pernambuco. I went there with my mom, step-dad and my baby brother!! :)

We stayed in a hotel/resort called Mana Beach Experience, it is a very nice and good looking place!

We went to several beaches, swim in pools, walked in rafts, visited the city center, and of course, we ate a lot :P

ABOUT PORTO DE GALINHAS:

Porto de Galinhas, in Pernambuco, Brazil, is famous for its clear waters, natural tide pools, and white sandy beaches. This charming destination offers snorkeling, diving, and vibrant local culture, making it a perfect tropical getaway.
